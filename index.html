Coronavirus what it is?

Coronavirus is a family of viruses, including
SARS-CoV-2, responsible for the COVID-19 pandemic. It causes respiratory illnesses ranging from mild to severe, and its symptoms may include fever, cough, and difficulty breathing. Vaccines and preventive measures are essential to control its spread.

What doctors say..

Doctors emphasize that COVID-19 is a serious viral illness that can cause severe respiratory issues. Vaccination, masks, and social distancing remain vital to limit spread. Timely testing and following public health guidelines are crucial for safeguarding public health.
